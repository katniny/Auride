Child Safety Policy

Last Updated: 4/17/2024


TransSocial recognizes the importance of protecting children and young people while using our platform. While we allow a diverse range of expression and sensitive topics, we are committed to providing safeguards and resources to help keep minors safe.


Age Restrictions

  • Users must be at least 13 years old to access TransSocial. We may require age verification procedures for users suspected to be under the minimum age in their jurisdiction.
  • Misrepresenting your age to access TransSocial is a serious violation of our Terms of Service.

    • Content Moderation

  • Proactive Measures: We employ a mix of automated tools and human moderation to identify and remove content that depicts or exploits minors in a sexually suggestive or harmful matter.

    • User Education

    Safety Resources: We provide links on our website with safety resources for young users, parents, and guardians. These include:

  • Tips for recognizing and reporting harmful content or interactions
  • Guidelines on online privacy and responsible social media use
  • Links to external support organizations

    • Parental Guidance

  • We strongly encourage parents and guardians to have open conversations with young people about safe and responsible online behavior.
  • We recommend parents to be aware of the social platforms their children use and familiarize themselves with available parental controls.

    • Additional Protection for Minors

  • Sensitivity Warnings: Content of a sensitive nature, even if allowed on TransSocial, must be clearly marked. This helps young users make informed choices about the content they see.

    • Collaboration with Law Enforcement

  • We cooperate with law enforcement agencies in investigations related to the suspected explication or abuse of minors.

    • Evolving Commitment

  • We recognize that protecting children online is an ongoing effort. We will continually review and update our safety measures in response to new technologies, potential risks, and user feedback.

    • Important Notes

  • Zero Tolerance: TransSocial has a zero-tolerance policy for any behavior that endangers, exploits, or abuses children. Offenders may face immediate account termination and be reported to authorities.
  • Transparency: We strive to be transparent in communicating our child safety policies and reporting on their effectiveness.
  • Challenges: Due to the nature of TransSocial allowing NSFW and sensitive content, fully protecting minors remains a challenge. This policy represents our best efforts to balance that with free expression.

    • Contact Us

    For any questions about our Child Safety Policy, or to report suspected abuse, please contact us at childsafety@transs.social
