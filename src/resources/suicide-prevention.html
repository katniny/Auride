Suicide Prevention Links

Created: 5/31/2025

Updated: Never


You're not alone.

If you're hurting, overwhelmed, or just need someone to talk to - please know you're not alone. You're deeply important, and there are people who care about you, even if it might not feel that way right now.


Whether you're struggling with your mental health, dealing with loss, or feeling like things are too heavy to carry alone -- reaching out is a strong and brave step.


Get help, no matter where you are

  • Worldwide: findahelpline.com - browse helplines by country and topic.
  • United States: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline - call or text 988 for free 24/7 support.
  • Canada: 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifelife (Canada) - Call 988 or text 988 for free 24/7 support.

Some gentle reminders

  • You're allowed to ask for help. No feeling is too small or unimportant.
  • Healing isn't linear. Good days and bad days can exist side by side.
  • You are not a burden -- your story matters.

If you're worried about someone else, let them know you're there. Even just a small message can mean everything.
